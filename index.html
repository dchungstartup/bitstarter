Gifts for All hopes to spread those feelings of surprise, fun and excitement you get when you receive a gift. Imagine the anticipation of getting an unexpected package, or even better, getting one every month! Maybe you only want to receive books, candy or fun little toys.. we'll make that happen for you. Or, maybe you just want to be surprised by the total randomness of receiving anything at all. We'll do that for you too :)

This is where we need your help. If this is going to get off the ground, we need to see that some of you out there think this would be just as fun as we think it'd be. Sign up and like us on Facebook, let your friends know. If you come, we'll build it :D

FAQ

How does it work?

Think of the fun and anticipation of receiving a surprise gift.

Now what if you had an easy way to give this to yourself or your friends? Maybe just as a one time thing, or maybe once a month, or maybe.. right out of the blue. You choose your budget and the sort of items you'd like, and we'll make the rest happen.
